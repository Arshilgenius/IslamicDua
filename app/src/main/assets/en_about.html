Hisnul Muslim

The book is a collection of dua'(supplications) for various occasions compiled by Dr. Sa'eed bin Ali bin Wahaf Al-Qahtani. The author is from amongst the senior students of the former Grand Mufti of Saudi Arabia, Shaykh Abdul Aziz bin Abdullah bin Baz (May Allah have mercy on him).

The App

After being dissatisfied with the quality of other Hisnul Muslim apps in the Play Store, it was about time to see if we could bring a much more refined experience to the scene.
